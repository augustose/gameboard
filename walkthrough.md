## ðŸ“¥ How to Install (PWA)

### iPhone (iOS)
1.  Open [iturix.web.app](https://iturix.web.app) in **Safari**.
2.  Tap the **Share** button (Square with arrow up) at the bottom.
3.  Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4.  Confirm the name and tap **Add**.
5.  Launch it from your home screen for a full-screen experience!

### Android (Chrome)
1.  Open the site in **Chrome**.
2.  You might see a specialized "Install" banner at the bottom.
3.  If not, tap the **Three Dots** menu (top right).
4.  Tap **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.
